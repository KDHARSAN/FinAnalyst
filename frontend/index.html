NLP Methods Used
TF-IDF Keyword Extraction
Term Frequency–Inverse Document Frequency ranks words by importance relative to the corpus. Used to extract key financial terms.
Sentiment Analysis
TextBlob lexicon-based polarity (-1 to +1) + subjectivity scoring. Cross-validated with domain-specific bullish/bearish word lexicons.
Extractive Summarization
Sentence scoring via word frequency distribution. Top-N sentences selected and reordered by document position.
Named Entity Recognition
Regex-based NER for financial entities: money values, company names (legal suffix matching), people (co-reference), dates and periods.
Extractive Q&A
Token overlap scoring between question and document sentences. Domain-specific routing for financial question types (revenue, risk, people, dates).
Risk Signal Classification
Dictionary-based detection with 3-tier classification (High/Medium/Low). Domain lexicon of 60+ financial risk terms.
